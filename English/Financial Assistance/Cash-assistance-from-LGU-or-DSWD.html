CASH ASSISTANCE FROM LGU OR DSWD

Here is a guide on the assistance available to registered Persons with Disabilities (PWDs) in Pasig City:

1. Pasig City Local Government Unit (LGU) Assistance

    Pasig City offers direct financial support, which is typically handled by the Persons with Disability Affairs Office (PDAO) and the City Social Welfare and Development Office (CSWDO).

    Annual Financial Assistance/Cash Gift:

    1. Pasig City has enacted Ordinance No. 11, Series of 2024, which institutionalizes an annual financial assistance for registered Pasig City PWDs.

    2. How to Avail: You must be a legitimate resident of Pasig City and a registered PWD with a valid Pasig City PWD ID. Contact the Pasig City PDAO for the exact schedule and procedure for the annual payout.

Other Pasig City CSWDO Assistance:

    The City Social Welfare and Development Office (CSWDO), in coordination with PDAO, may offer other forms of financial or material assistance for specific crisis situations, such as medical or burial aid, which you can inquire about at the City Hall.

    2. DSWD Assistance

    The DSWD provides financial aid through its core social safety net programs.

    Assistance to Individuals in Crisis Situation (AICS):
    AICS is the DSWD's program that provides various forms of assistance, including medical, burial, educational, transportation, and cash assistance for other support services, for individuals and families who are in crisis situations.

How to Avail:

    1. Go to the nearest DSWD Crisis Intervention Unit (CIU) or DSWD Field Office (for Metro Manila, this would be DSWD-NCR).

    2. Screening and Interview: A social worker will conduct an interview and assessment to determine your eligibility and the appropriate type and amount of assistance.

    3. Requirements: While general AICS requirements are broad, for a PWD requesting aid (e.g., for medicine or an assistive device), you will typically need:

    - Any valid ID (yours and a representative's, if applicable).
    - PWD ID (duly issued by the Pasig PDAO).
    - Medical Certificate or Clinical Abstract with a diagnosis (issued within the last 3 months, with the physician's complete name, license number, and signature).
    - Prescription (for medicine) or Official Quotation/Statement of Account (for hospital bill, device, etc.).

    For more valid information, contact or go to their sites:
    DSWD AICS Official Website: //aics.dswd.gov.ph/
    Contact Numbers (CIU): 8962-2813 / 8951-7433
    Email: inquiry@dswd.gov.ph

Steps to Secure Assistance

    1. Register as a PWD: Ensure you are a registered PWD in Pasig City. The Pasig City PDAO processes the application and issuance of the PWD ID.
    PDAO Email: pasigcitypdao@gmail.com

    2. Contact PDAO/CSWDO: Call or visit the Pasig City PDAO or CSWDO to inquire about their specific cash gift schedule and any other local financial or medical assistance they currently offer.

    3. Check DSWD CIU: For emergency or crisis-specific financial/medical aid, visit the nearest DSWD Crisis Intervention Unit (CIU) for an AICS social worker assessment.
