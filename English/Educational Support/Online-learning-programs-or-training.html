NATIONAL ACCESSIBLE ONLINE LEARNING SYSTEMS (NCDA & TESDA)

online learning programs accessible nationwide online platforms for PWDs.

Program/Platform Description Key Features & Accessibility
National Council on Disability Affairs (NCDA) - Accessible Online Learning System (AOLS) A dedicated, governmentrun platform offering free, accessible courses to promote disability awareness and capacitybuilding for PWDs and support personnel. FREE, ONLINE, SELF-PACED, ACCESSIBLE. Offers courses on: Disability Sensitivity, PDAO-Related Topics, Civil Service Exam Review, and Gender and Development. Upon successful completion, learners receive a governmentissued, verified Certificate of Completion.
For more information, direct to their official websites: https://ncdacourses.online/
TESDA Online Program (TOP) The webbased platform of the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA) that offers free Massive Open Online Courses (MOOCs) for technical education and skills development (TVET). FREE, ONLINE, SELF-PACED. PWDs are specifically prioritized for TESDA scholarships for full qualifications. Selected courses (e.g., Food Processing NC II, Bread and Pastry Production NC II) have integrated accessibility features like https://e-tesda.gov.ph/ Filipino Sign Language and screen-reading capabilities.
For more information, direct to their official websites: https://e-tesda.gov.ph/
UPOU Massive Open Distance eLearning (MODeL) The official MOOC platform of the University of the Philippines Open University. Offers various academic and professional courses. FREE, SELFPACED. Committed to Universal Accessibility (UA), with courses designed using Open Educational Resources (OERs) to cater to diverse learning styles.
For more information, direct to their official websites: https://model.upou.edu.ph/
