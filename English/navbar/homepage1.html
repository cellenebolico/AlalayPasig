How AlalayPasig Helps PWDs

Our platform is designed to make finding and accessing services easier for Persons with Disabilities in Pasig City.

Healthcare Services

Connect with specialized healthcare providers, therapy services, and medical equipment suppliers.

Employment Assistance

Find job opportunities, training programs, and workplace accommodations for persons with disabilities.

Educational Support

Access special education resources, learning materials, and academic assistance programs.

Housing Resources

Discover accessible housing options, home modification service.

Community Support

Join support groups, community activities, and social events for persons with disabilities.


How AlalayPasig Works

Our platform makes it easy to find and connect with the services you need in just a few simple steps.

Create Your Profile

Sign up and create your personalized profile by providing information about your specific needs and preferences.

Discover Services

Browse through our comprehensive directory of services available in Pasig City or receive personalized recommendations.

Connect & Engage

Reach out to service providers directly through our platform and schedule appointments or consultations.

Rate & Review

Share your experiences to help other PWDs make informed decisions and improve service quality in our community.
