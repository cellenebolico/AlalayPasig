PRIVACY AND POLICY

Privacy and Policy - AlalayPasig:


    Last Updated: 10/10/2025

    AlalayPasig is committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of Persons with
    Disabilities (PWDs) and all users who access our platform. This Privacy Policy explains
    how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

    By using our website, you agree to the practices described below.



    What We Collect

    We may collect the following personal information:

    • Name, email address, and contact number (if voluntarily provided)
    • Location data (for service mapping)
    • Feedback, suggestions, or form submissions
    • Usage data (pages visited and device type)

    We do not collect sensitive personal data unless explicitly required for service
    delivery and with your consent.

    Why We Collect It

    We collect data to:

    • Connect you with relevant services and support
    • Improve accessibility and user experience
    • Respond to inquiries or feedback
    • Monitor platform performance and usage trends

    Consent and Control

    You have full control over your data. You may:

    • Request access to your personal data
    • Ask for corrections or updates
    • Withdraw consent or request deletion

    To exercise these rights, contact us at alalaypasig@gmail.com

    Data Protection Measures

    We implement organizational, physical, and technical safeguards to protect your data.
    These include:

    • Secure servers and encrypted connections
    • Limited access to authorized personnel
    • Regular audits and compliance checks

    Data Sharing

    We do not sell your data. We may share it with:

    • Government agencies or NGOs for service coordination
    • Trusted partners who follow the same privacy standards

    All sharing is done with your prior consent or as required by law.

    Cookies and Analytics

    We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. These may track:

    • Session activity
    • Device type
    • Preferences

    You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not
    work properly.

    Data Retention

    We retain your data only as long as needed for service delivery or legal compliance.
    After that, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

    Legal Compliance

    AlalayPasig complies with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 and all relevant laws and
    regulations in the Philippines concerning the protection of personal data
    We ensure that:

    • Personal data is collected and processed lawfully, fairly, and transparently
    • Users are informed of their rights and how to exercise them
    • Data is secured using appropriate technical and organizational measures
    • Any sharing of data is done with consent or legal basis

    Our commitment to legal compliance reflects our dedication to protecting the dignity,
    privacy, and autonomy of all users—especially Persons with Disabilities (PWDs) who
    rely on AlalayPasig for access to vital services.

    Contact Us

    For privacy concerns, data requests, or feedback, reach out to:
    Email: alalaypasig@gmail.com
    Office: Maybunga Pasig City.
