Our Mission

Our mission is to empower Persons with Disabilities(PWDs) and their families in Pasig City by providing an accessible and efficient web-based platform. We aim to connect them seamlessly with the diverse programs and services available, ensuring they can easily find the assistance they need and deserve, fostering a more inclusive and supportive community.

Our Beliefs and Core Values

where no one is left behind

At Alalay Pasig, we believe that unity and understanding are key to build a community.

Our Team

The Alalay Pasig team is composed of dedicated individuals with diverse skills, backgrounds, and experiences, all united by a singular purpose to empower Persons with Disabilities in Pasig City and build a more inclusive community through accessible technology and heartfelt service.

Our Founders: GROW AN ENEMY

The founders of Alalay Pasig—the dedicated members of GROW AN ENEMY— came together with a singular, powerful vision: to transform service access for Persons with Disabilities (PWDs) in Pasig City. By combining their unique skills and expertise in accessible technology, community development, and innovative system design, they established this web-based platform. Their goal is clear: to ensure that every PWD can easily find the support they need, embodying the collective spirit of their group and the belief that no one is left behind.
