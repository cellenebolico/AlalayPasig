ACCESSIBILITY STATEMENT

Accessibility Statement - AlalayPasig:


    Last Updated: 10/10/2025

    AlalayPasig is committed to making our digital platform accessible to all users,
    especially Persons with Disabilities (PWDs). We believe that access to information and
    services is a right—not a privilege—and we strive to ensure that our website reflects
    this principle.



    Inclusive Design

    We designed AlalayPasig with accessibility at its core. Our features include:
    • Screen reader compatibility
    • Keyboard navigation support
    • Voice command
    • Clear, simple language in both English and Tagalog
    • Mobile-friendly layout for assistive devices

    Community-Centered Approach

    We consulted with PWD advocates, caregivers, and local organizations to ensure our
    platform reflects real needs. We welcome feedback from users to continuously
    improve accessibility.

    Ongoing Improvements

    Accessibility is an ongoing process. We regularly test our platform using:

    • WCAG 2.1 guidelines
    • Manual and automated audits
    • User feedback from PWD communities

    If you encounter any barriers while using AlalayPasig, please let us know.

    Contact for Accessibility Support

    For accessibility concerns, suggestions, or assistance:
    Email: pdao@pasigcity.gov.ph
    Office: Pasig City Hall, Persons with Disability Affairs Office (PDAO)
