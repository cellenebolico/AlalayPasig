WHEELCHAIR OR CRUTCHES

How to Request a Free Wheelchair or Crutches (Pasig City)

    To avail of free assistive devices from the Pasig City LGU, you must apply through the PDAO and meet certain criteria, particularly those related to indigency and residency.

Application Requirements

    While PDAO program requirements may vary depending on the specific distribution drive (e.g., if it is in partnership with a specific foundation), the standard documents you generally need to submit are:

    Photocopy of PWD ID or Senior Citizen ID.
    Printed Latest Whole Body Picture (that shows the disability, if applicable).
    Social Case Study from the Pasig City Office on Social Welfare and Development(OSWD).
    Barangay Certificate of Indigency (proof of indigency and residence).

Application Process

    1. Contact PDAO: Reach out to the Pasig City PDAO to inquire about the next scheduled distribution or application period for assistive devices. These programs are often announced on their official social media pages.
    2. Submit Requirements: Submit the required documents (PWD ID copy, whole body picture, Social Case Study, and Certificate of Indigency) to the PDAO office located in the Pasig City Hall Complex.
    3. Assessment and Validation: The PDAO will assess the applicant's disability and need to determine the appropriate device and qualification for the program.
    4. Wait for Schedule: If approved, the applicant will be scheduled for the distribution and fitting of the assistive device (wheelchair, crutches, prosthetic leg, etc.).

Pasig City PDAO Contact Information

    For the most current information regarding the requirements and the next schedule for the distribution of free wheelchairs and crutches, it is best to directly contact the Pasig City PDAO.

    Location: GAD Compound beside SWAC and OSCA, Pasig City Hall Complex, Caruncho Ave., Barangay San Nicolas, Pasig City.
    Mobile: 09562535487
    Email: pdao@pasigcity.gov.ph
